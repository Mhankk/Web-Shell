Web Defacement
By.Mh@nkk

Deface is an attack on a website with the aim of changing the contents of a website with the aim of sending a certain message, or just showing its capabilities.

Usually, the messages given by the defacer are in various scopes such as political, religious, or inappropriate words with the aim of embarrassing the attacked website or organization.

Website defacement does not always have bad intentions, one of which is website security testing carried out by third parties unilaterally. Even so, important data on the website can also be the target of deface attacks.

This of course will be more detrimental if the website that is attacked belongs to an important company or organization. Therefore, security is something that cannot be compromised – even more so for websites that store visitor data or financial information.
